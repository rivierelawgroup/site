He is AV rated by Martindale-Hubbell which is the "Highest Possible Rating in Both Legal Ability and Ethical Standards". Roger was also named a "Top 100 Litigation Lawyer" in Arizona by The American Society of Legal Advocates, "10 Best 2016 for Client Satisfaction" by The American Institute of Personal Injury Lawyers, received the 2015 "Client Distinction Award" from Martindale Hubbell, and has been named a "Top Rated Lawyer" by The Arizona Republic.
